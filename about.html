About the Author

Cheryl Spanos writes short stories and fantasy novels for children and adults. Her fiction has been published in Stories for Children Magazine, Hunger Mountain, Beyond Centauri, and the California Writers Club Literary Review. Her short stories have won honorable mention in the Annual Writer’s Digest Writing Competition. Her manuscript for a middle-grade fantasy novel, “Tougher Than Dragon Hide,” was awarded honorable mention for children’s fiction at the 2008 San Francisco Writers Conference. She is a member of the Society of Children’s Book Writers and Illustrators (SCBWI) and the Mount Diablo branch of the California Writers Club.
